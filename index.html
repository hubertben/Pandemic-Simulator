What is this?

Over the past few months, the planet has been under the harsh outbreak of the COVID-19 Virus. This simulation was built in order to demonstrate the importance of Social Distancing, Wearing a Mask, and Washing your Hands.

Statistics from the simulation will be provided at the end of the simulation. Enjoy :)



Simulation Adjustment Controls

Social Distancing

Adjusting the Social Distancing slider will allow for simulated humans to stay further apart from one another

Mask Usage

Adjusting the Mask Usage slider will prepare simulated humans with a mask at the start of the simulation casuing less of a spread

Hand Washing

Adjusting the Hand Washing slider will allow simulated humans to wash their hands after an interaction with another simulated human
